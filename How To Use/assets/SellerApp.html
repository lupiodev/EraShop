Introduction

  • Hey there, This is for erashop seller app documentation to know the app in better way and how to use it.

Become Seller

  • User profile screen shown to become seller option.
  • Below is the screen.
  • click on become seller option and open become seller screen and fill the all correct Details.
  • click on Next button and open otp screen enter correct otp and sent request to admin.
  • click on continue button and open address detail page fill correct detail.
  • click on Next button and open to account details page fill correct detail.
  • click on next button and see terms & condition.
  • click to continue and sent seller request to admin.
  • Admin can accept seller request to admin panel and you become seller.

Splash screen

  • This is EraShop seller app splash screen.When user successfully login to app redirects to this page by default same as User App.
  • Below is the screen.
  • All live seller with their name ,image ,New Collection product,shorts can be seen with searching in this screen same as user App.

Feed

  • By clicking on fourth icon in bottom navbar, seller profile and seller name and verified icon can be seen.
  • click on seller name and seller profile opens.
  • There is an "edit" button suggests sellers can edit their shop profile information.
  • There is a section titled catalog which allows sellers to add, edit, and manage their product listings.
  • There is a section titled my orders which allows sellers to track and manage customer orders.
  • There is a section titled my wallet which allows sellers to view their sales history and manage their account balance.
  • There is a section titled livestemming which related to livestreaming functionality where sellers can showcase and sell products directly through live streams.

Add Catalog

  • click on catalog and open catalog page.
  • Add product by click on + button.
  • Click to add catalog and see to this seller product.

My Order​

  • click on my order and all order page open.
  • To view pending order click on pending.
  • To view confirm order click on confirm.
  • And show this order in confirm order.
  • Click to confirm order.
  • And show this order in out of delivery order.
  • Click to change order status out of delivery.
  • And show this order in delivered order.

My Wallet

  • click on My Wallet seller can view all earning ,pending order amount and delivered order amount.

Live Streaming

  • click on live streaming and see catalog page.
  • seller can select catalog and go live stream.

Shorts

  • click on shorts and seller can add new short .
  • Fill correct detail to below the page.
  • click to upload short.

Address & Bank Account

  • click on my address to view seller address details.
  • Click to change address button and change address.
  • Same as click to bank account and show bank account details.
  • Click to change bank detail button and change bank detail.
  • Click to save changes and save bank details.
